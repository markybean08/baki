NEWS & ANNOUNCEMENT

  • **Celebrating Student Achievements!**

    We are thrilled to announce that students from the College of Engineering of Cor Jesu College showcased their innovative research at the prestigious 4th International Conference on Engineering, Social-Sciences, and Humanities (ICESSU-2024)!

    **Computer Engineering Presentation:**

    Jared Gamutin captivated the audience with his paper on "Air Purifier with Thermoelectric Cooling and Advance Air Monitoring Sensors." His groundbreaking research promises advancements in air quality control and monitoring, utilizing cutting-edge technology to enhance environmental sustainability.
